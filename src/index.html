FIL Lisbon will take place from Oct 30 - Nov 4, with main stage days on Oct 31 & Nov 1 at LX Factory: A two-day event with insightful talks from top-notch speakers, panel discussions from the world's foremost Filecoin ecosystem participants and Web3 co-builders. Our unmatched speaker lineup will dive into Filecoin and Web3, taking you through the Filecoin world of FVM, NFTs, the metaverse, and beyond to be a part of the Web3 wave.

There are talks, meetups, workshops, hackathons & happy hours, together with music and fun across the city to bring together developers, storage providers, ecosystem partners, investors, and clients across the whole blockchain industry. Let's connect in Lisbon!
