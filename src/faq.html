Frequently Asked Questions
What else is happening in Lisbon?

There are loads of events planned as part of Lisbon blockchain month. Learn more at their website.

Where do I stay in Lisbon?

Lisbon is a very accessible city, with an abundance of hotels, guest houses and B&Bs! If you need help booking, reach out to events@fil.org.

How do I get around Lisbon?

LX Factory is easily reachable by tram, taxi, or on foot if you’re an explorer! And if you’re stuck, you can always flag down a rickshaw.
